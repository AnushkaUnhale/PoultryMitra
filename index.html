Every Year, Thousands of Chickens in India Don't Survive

This alarming statistic isn't just a matter of economic loss or a blow to poultry farming; it's a wake-up call highlighting the dire need for improved health standards and care within avian agricultural practices. The widespread mortality of chickens not only impacts the livelihoods of countless farmers, relying heavily on poultry for income, but it also signals potential threats to public health through the possible transmission of avian diseases. Addressing this issue is crucial, not only for the welfare of millions of chickens but also for the economic stability of those who depend on poultry farming and for the broader goal of ensuring food safety and security.
